How This Prototype Works

  • Click tiles to assemble the word on the workbench.
  • Prefixes belong on the right side of a Hebrew word and suffixes belong on the left.
  • You get points for correct builds and bonus streak points for back-to-back success.
  • This is a fast proof of concept, so the content set is intentionally small and editable.
